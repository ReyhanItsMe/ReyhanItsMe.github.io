Pengertian

Teorema Pythagoras menyatakan bahwa dalam segitiga siku-siku, kuadrat sisi miring sama dengan jumlah kuadrat dari dua sisi lainnya.

Rumus Utama

a² + b² = c²

c = Sisi Miring (Hypotenuse)

Contoh Soal

"Jika lebar buku (a) = 6cm dan tinggi (b) = 8cm, berapakah panjang diagonalnya (c)?"

1 c² = 6² + 8²
2 c² = 36 + 64 = 100
Hasil: 10 cm! ✨
Tips & Trick

Gunakan Triple Pythagoras (3, 4, 5) untuk hitungan cepat! Cukup kalikan 2 jadi (6, 8, 10).

Definition

The Pythagorean theorem states that in a right-angled triangle, the square of the hypotenuse is equal to the sum of the squares of the other two sides.

Core Formula

a² + b² = c²

c = The Hypotenuse (Longest Side)

Practice Problem

"If a book's width (a) = 6cm and height (b) = 8cm, calculate the length of its diagonal (c)."

1 c² = 6² + 8²
2 c² = 36 + 64 = 100
Result: 10 cm! ✨
Quick Trick

Use Pythagorean Triples (3, 4, 5) for fast counting! Simply multiply by 2 to get (6, 8, 10).
